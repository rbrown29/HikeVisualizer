Multnomah-Wahkeena Loop Hike



Wahkeena Falls Loop is a beautiful experience located in the Columbia River Gorge National Scenic Area that brings hikers to one of the best waterfalls on the Oregon side. Start from the Wahkeena Picnic Area and take Wahkeena which passes by several waterfalls and ascends Wahkeena Creek past the springs which feed the waterfall
